ABOUT

Connect Open Source is an organization which is working towards bringing global Open Source communities together on one platform.

flash_on

What we aim for?

We aim to join hands with different global and local Open Source Communities/Projects and help them in achieving their goals by supporting the Opensource eco-system. We as an Organization want to set a platform which lets volunteers explore different opportunities they have for them and also encourage new Open Source startups across the globe.

group

How do we work?

We work to connect opensource enthusiasts with right opensource communities/projects and spread the love of opensource by organizing cross platform events,providing free spaces and support opensource startups and much more

settings

Who we are?

We’re a community with seven different teams where people can volunteer according to their abilities. The teams are Tech, Documentation, Marketing, Campus connections, Community Operations, Design and Spaces evangelists.
